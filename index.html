Hi! I'm Samantha, a graphic designer with a passion for creativity and innovation. My approach to design is centered on the balance between aesthetics and functionality, ensuring that each project not only looks extraordinary but also communicates the right message.


I firmly believe that collaboration and communication are essential for the success of any project, and I am committed to building strong relationships with my clients to fully understand their needs and aspirations.

Accademia delle Alte Tecnologie - Master in Web and Graphic Design

2024

I completed a Master in Web and Graphic Design, where I deepened my knowledge and skills in the following areas

  • Web Design: Design and development of responsive and user-friendly websites, with a particular focus on user experience (UX) and user interface (UI).
  • Web Graphics: Development of web-optimized graphic elements, including banners, icons, and vector images.
  • Prototyping and Wireframing: Use of tools such as Figma to create interactive prototypes and detailed wireframes.
  • Collaborative Projects: Participation in group projects, working with other designers and developers to create integrated and innovative web solutions.

This master's program allowed me to combine graphic design skills with web design expertise, preparing me to develop comprehensive and functional visual solutions for the digital world.

Academy of Fine Arts, Graphic Design

2020-2022

I started a course of study in Graphic Design at the Academy of Fine Arts, where I acquired a solid foundation in the following areas

  • Graphic Design: Development of visual concepts for a variety of media, including print, web, and multimedia.
  • Typography: Study of typographic principles and practical application in creating appealing and readable layouts.
  • Design Software: Advanced proficiency in using software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
  • Color Theory: Understanding color combinations and their emotional and visual impact.
  • Branding and Visual Identity: Creation of logos, brands, and guidelines for the coherent visual identity of companies and products.
  • Workshops and Practical Projects: Participation in workshops, labs, and collaborative projects to develop practical skills and work in teams.
